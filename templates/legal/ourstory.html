Our Story

At eKhoji Nepal, we believe in the power of local commerce. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a platform where Nepali businesses and customers could connect effortlessly within their own communities.

How It All Started

In a rapidly digitalizing world, we noticed a gap in the market. While global e-commerce giants were expanding their reach, local businesses in Nepal were struggling to establish their digital presence. Many small business owners with exceptional products and services lacked the resources and technical knowledge to bring their offerings online.

That's when the idea of eKhoji was born—a platform specifically designed to empower local sellers and provide customers with easy access to products and services in their vicinity.

Our Mission

To strengthen local economies across Nepal by bridging the gap between neighborhood businesses and their communities through technology.

We're committed to:

  • Creating opportunities for local businesses to thrive in the digital marketplace
  • Providing Nepali consumers with convenient access to products and services near them
  • Building sustainable communities through local commerce
  • Supporting the growth of small and medium enterprises throughout Nepal

Our Vision

We envision a Nepal where:

  • Every business, regardless of size, has the opportunity to establish a strong online presence
  • Consumers can easily discover and purchase from businesses in their local area
  • Communities are strengthened through sustainable local commerce
  • The unique products and services of each region are celebrated and accessible

What Sets Us Apart

Unlike other e-commerce platforms, eKhoji Nepal focuses exclusively on local connections. We're not just another marketplace—we're a community-building tool that:

  • Prioritizes proximity, connecting buyers with sellers in their own neighborhoods
  • Simplifies the digital transition for traditional businesses
  • Promotes local entrepreneurship and economic resilience
  • Reduces delivery times and environmental impact through shorter supply chains
  • Preserves the unique character of Nepal's diverse communities

Join the Local Movement

Whether you're a business owner looking to expand your reach or a consumer seeking quality products and services near you, eKhoji Nepal invites you to be part of our story. Together, we can create thriving local economies across Nepal, one neighborhood at a time.
